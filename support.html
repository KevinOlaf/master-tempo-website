Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an Android version?

Currently, Master Tempo Pro is available exclusively for iOS. This allows us to leverage specific hardware features like the Taptic Engine and Core Audio for maximum precision.

How do I change the beat sound?

Tap the sound icon in the bottom toolbar to cycle through our 4 studio-grade sound modes: Classic, Woodblock, Hi-Hat, and Snap.

Can I use the metronome while using other apps?

Yes! Master Tempo Pro supports background play. You can lock your screen or switch to another app (like a sheet music reader), and the metronome will continue running.

How do I delete my data?

Master Tempo Pro does not collect any personal data or create user accounts. All your settings (BPM preferences, themes) are stored locally on your device. Simply deleting the app will remove all local data.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

We appreciate your help in improving the app. Please use the "Contact Support" button above to send us an email with details about the issue, your device model, and iOS version.
